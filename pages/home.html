FITELTY

01

Introduction

Fitelty is a service that reads a person's heart rate, blood pressure and oxygen levels from their smart watch and automatically recommends a personalised lifestyle for better health

02

Purpose

It will automatically recommend a diet, sleep schedule and exercise schedule based on factors like the person's heart rate, blood pressure, age, height, weight, etc.

03

Features

Users will be notified if their blood pressure is too high or low. The nearest hospital will be informed in case of a heart attack or emergency. Users can choose the intensity of their training as well.
